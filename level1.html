What This Level Covers

What Is Inside Level 1.

Level 1 is called Financial Foundations for a reason. You cannot build a house on sand and you cannot build wealth on broken financial habits and an unexamined mindset. This program goes to the root.

Most people who struggle financially are not lacking information. They are lacking the discipline, accountability, and foundational financial awareness to make information stick. Level 1 installs that foundation.

Every module, every checkpoint, and every accountability measure in Level 1 is designed to do one thing. Build the habits, beliefs, and behaviors that make everything else possible.
