A Message From Darrell

You Should Not Be Doing This Alone.

When I was learning about financial literacy in the Marine Corps, the thing that made the biggest difference was not just the information. It was being around other people who were taking it seriously. People who were asking the same questions. Working toward the same goals. Holding each other accountable.

Most people try to figure out money in isolation. They watch videos. They read articles. They take notes. And then life happens and the momentum fades because there is nobody around them to keep it going.

The RSM Wealth Builders Community exists to change that. It is a place where financial truth is spoken daily, where questions get real answers, where wins get celebrated, and where you are never the only one working toward something better.

It is free. It is real. And it is waiting for you. Come join us.

Community Standards
01
Truth over comfort. We tell each other the truth here even when it is uncomfortable. This is a judgment-free space but it is not a comfort zone.
02
No pitching or selling. This community is for learning and support, not for selling products, services, or opportunities to other members.
03
Celebrate others. When a community member shares a win you celebrate it fully. Rising tides lift all ships. Someone else's success does not diminish yours.
04
Show up consistently. The value of community comes from consistency. Post. Comment. Engage. Your presence matters and your journey inspires others.
05
Confidentiality. Financial conversations are personal. What is shared in the community stays in the community.
06
Do the work. This community supports action. We are here to build something real. Come ready to learn, apply, and grow.
