Why This Matters

Why This Topic Changes Audiences.

Schools teach students how to earn a living. They do not teach students what to do with that living once they earn it. Students graduate knowing algebra and history but nothing about credit, debt, compound interest, investing, or the mindset required to build any kind of financial stability. This presentation closes that gap in a way that is real, relatable, and immediately applicable.

What Is Covered

Inside the Presentation.

01
What Money Actually Is
Most students have never been taught the basics of how money moves, how it grows, and how decisions made at 18 echo for decades. This section starts at the true beginning.
02
Credit, Debt, and Compound Interest
Plain language explanations of the financial tools that will shape every young person's life whether they understand them or not. Darrell makes sure they understand them.
03
The Mindset That Determines Financial Outcomes
Before strategy comes thinking. This section gives students a framework for thinking about money that will serve them for the rest of their lives.
04
Practical First Steps They Can Take Right Now
Darrell does not leave young people with inspiration and no direction. Every student leaves with concrete actions they can take immediately regardless of their current financial situation.
