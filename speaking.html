Keynote Topics

Four Talks. One Mission.

Each topic is available as a keynote, workshop, or customized session. All are adaptable for your specific audience, format, and time constraints.

01
Signature Keynote
The Right Side of Money

Darrell's most requested presentation. A complete journey from the root of financial struggle all the way through to a clear, practical framework for building real wealth. Delivered with honesty, urgency, and real-world application that sticks long after the applause stops.

Audience Takeaways
Understand why financial struggle is a foundation problem, not an income problem
Identify the money beliefs silently driving their financial outcomes
Walk away with one immediate action step toward financial clarity
02
Schools and Youth
What They Never Taught You: Financial Truth for the Next Generation

An honest, engaging, age-appropriate conversation about money that students almost never hear in a classroom. Darrell speaks directly to young people about credit, debt, compound interest, and the wealth building habits that start before the first paycheck.

Audience Takeaways
Understand credit, debt, and compound interest in plain language
Learn why mindset matters before income does
Leave with a simple financial starting point they can implement immediately
03
Corporate and HR
Financial Wellness from the Inside Out

Financial stress is one of the leading drivers of reduced workplace productivity, disengagement, and turnover. This workshop goes beyond surface-level tips and addresses the root causes, providing practical tools and mindset shifts that improve both personal finances and organizational performance.

Audience Takeaways
Identify how financial stress impacts workplace performance
Build awareness of the most common financial patterns that keep employees stuck
Walk away with a practical framework they can implement immediately
04
Community Organizations
Breaking the Cycle: Generational Wealth Starts Here

This presentation speaks directly to communities where financial struggle has been a generational experience, not because of lack of effort, but because of lack of access to the right knowledge. Delivered with compassion, conviction, and the authenticity of someone who has lived the experience.

Audience Takeaways
Understand the generational cycle of financial struggle and how it is broken
Learn the mindset shifts required before financial behavior can change
Discover practical first steps toward building a legacy
