About the Book

The Book Most People Needed and Never Got.

Most people were never taught how money actually works. Not in school. Not at home. Not anywhere. They were handed a formula — go to school, get a job, work hard, survive — and told that was the path. Nobody talked about the mindset. Nobody talked about the beliefs that silently drive every financial decision a person makes long before they ever earn their first dollar.

Creating A Wealth Building Mindset was written to fill that gap. Not with theory. Not with textbook formulas. With the hard-earned, lived truth of someone who grew up on the South Side of Chicago, joined the Marine Corps, struggled financially despite working hard, and then discovered the missing piece that changed everything.

The missing piece was never income. It was always foundation. The mindset. The financial literacy. The beliefs that need to be rebuilt before any strategy, investment, or habit can actually stick.

This book starts at the root and works its way up. Chapter by chapter it replaces the financial beliefs that keep people stuck with the ones that actually build wealth. It is honest. It is direct. And it is the conversation your parents could not have with you because nobody had it with them.

About the Author

Darrell Thompson Did Not Write This Book Because He Had It Figured Out.

He wrote it because he needed it to exist. Growing up on the South Side of Chicago, Darrell was surrounded by hard work and determination but not financial education. He joined the United States Marine Corps, served his country with discipline and commitment, and still found himself struggling financially as a young adult.

Then he attended a Financial Specialist course in the Corps and everything changed. Not because the information was complicated. Because it was so clear and nobody had ever given it to him before. That moment turned Darrell into a lifelong student of financial literacy and mindset, and he has spent every year since helping others close the gap that was closed for him in that room.

Today Darrell is a licensed real estate professional, a landlord, a licensed life and health insurance producer, a College Adjunct Professor, the founder of Right Side of Money and TNT Legacy Shield, and a financial literacy speaker and coach. Everything he teaches comes from something he has personally lived.
